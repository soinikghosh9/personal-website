About Me

I am a PhD researcher in Biomedical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Banaras Hindu University (IIT BHU), India, with a background in Physiology (B.Sc & M.Sc, University of Calcutta). My work bridges Computational Neuroscience, AI, Cognitive Science, Biology, Psychology, BCI, Neurorehabilitation, and Medical Technology. Currently developing an EEG-based AI algorithm for seizure forecasting, alongside projects in prosthetic control via motor imagery, AI-powered ultrasound imaging, VR-based robotic physiotherapy, and bioinformatics.

Equally wired into music, film, and art, I play multiple instruments, compose, shoot and explore how creativity heals. Passionate about brain-body connection, psychology, generative AI, and the mysteries of the mind. I learn something new every day and believe in open minds, open hearts, and building a better future.
